Stories of Moo Moo

What make Moo the best dog ever was he was so low maintance. For a little dog he was not barky, very affectionate, playful, full of personality and at times spunky. He was always at the door to great you when you arrived home and suggle with you the moment you sat down. He loved sleeping under the covers with you and did not move the entire night. One of his favorite things to do was sun bathing by the screen door when the sun was shinning through. He tolerated getting dressed, grooming and bath time. Overall he was a trooper about everything we tried with him.

Moo had all these secrete spots he loved to him in and we were forever calling him to see where he would appear up next. One time he caming running down the stairs sporting one of my sons undies half draped on him it was the new fashion statement and had the whole family laughing up a storm! He loved his stuffed animals which at times would be bigger then him. But his very favorite was Piggie, he would carry him around all the time, even after many washings Piggie still looked filty dirty. But we didn't have the heart to throw it for we knew Moo would be looing for him for days. Here is a short Movie my oldest son made of Moo and Piggie.
